By Yves Wasnyo MD, PhDc

A real-time environmental health monitoring platform for urban Cameroon. Integrates AirQo sensor networks and OpenWeatherMap One Call 3.0 to display air quality, meteorological hazards, and composite risk scores across Douala and Yaoundé.
